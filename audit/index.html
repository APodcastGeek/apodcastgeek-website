What we diagnose

The 5 places B2B podcasts leak the most

Drawn from auditing 50+ B2B shows over the past three years. Every leak ties to a specific revenue or pipeline outcome.

01
Strategy & Positioning

No defined ICP. No North Star. Episodes drift between topics no one is searching for.

02
Production Quality

Weak hooks, no episode structure, audio that signals amateur in the first 15 seconds.

03
Audience & Distribution

Only on Spotify. No video. No clips. The 90% of attention living on YouTube and LinkedIn is invisible to you.

04
Monetisation & ROI

No ad strategy. No guest funnel. The conversation ends when the episode ends.

05
Pipeline Tracking

No system to attribute deals back to episodes. You can't justify the spend so the budget gets cut.

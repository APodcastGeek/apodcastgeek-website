Who this is for

Is This Right for You?

The APG Brand Builder is built for B2B founders and expert brands who take their podcast seriously.

This is for you if:

  • You're a B2B founder who wants your podcast to generate pipeline and revenue
  • You want decision makers in your industry sitting across from your microphone
  • You want world-class production without building an internal team
  • You want to show up, record, and have everything else handled
  • You measure success in deals and relationships, not download numbers

This is not for you if:

  • You're looking for the cheapest production option
  • You want to manage your own guests and production workflow
  • You see podcasting as a hobby, not a business tool
  • You're not ready to commit to consistent monthly episodes
